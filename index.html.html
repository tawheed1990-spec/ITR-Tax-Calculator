Salary income

Your entire annual salary package before deductions. Found on Form-16 Part B.
Only a few allowances stay tax-free under the new regime:
  • Official travel reimbursements
  • Outstation daily allowance
  • Conveyance for official duty
  • Divyang / disability transport benefit (max Rs. 38,400/yr)
